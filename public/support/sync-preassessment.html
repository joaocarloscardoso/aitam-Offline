When assessing risk, GUID 5100/5.11.13 must be taken into account.
The risk viewed at plan matrix and generated from the sync operation with pre-assessment (namely, A2.02 matrix “Understanding the IT-systems”) is based on a two-step analysis:

  1. Find a draft risk evaluation for the domain in global (RED), based on general questions:
     
    General questions for domain:
    GQ01 Responsibilities defined (Admitted values: High = 3, Medium = 2, Low = 1)
    GQ02 Adequate structure (Admitted values: High = 3, Medium = 2, Low = 1)
    GQ03 Sufficient regulation (Admitted values: High = 3, Medium = 2, Low = 1)
    GQ04 Explanatory and clear documentation (Admitted values: High = 3, Medium = 2, Low = 1)
     
    RED = [(GQ01 + GQ02 + GQ03 + GQ04) / 4]
     
    Applies this RED value as a generic risk evaluation for all issues, inside areas of evaluated of domain. Risk value is documented at “Reasons, risks and remarks” column.
     
  2. Find a draft risk evaluation for each area in the domain (Admitted values: High = 3, Medium = 2, Low = 1):
     
    Rewrites risk evaluation for all issues inside, based on specific value found for evaluated area. Risk value is documented at “Reasons, risks and remarks” column.
    Step 2 takes precedence over step 1.
